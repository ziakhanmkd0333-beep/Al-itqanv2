Noorani Qaida Course

A beginner-level course designed for students who want to start learning how to read the Holy Quran correctly. Focuses on basic Arabic alphabet, correct pronunciation (Makharij), joining letters, and simple reading rules. Suitable for children, beginners, and new learners with no prior Arabic knowledge.
